THE PERSON BEHIND BRITS

Meet the Founder

Brits Solutions was built on a simple frustration: buying hardware for short-term needs makes no sense. Here's who's behind fixing that.

Roop Singh Pal
Founder & Director, Brits Solutions
"Reliable Devices. Flexible Rentals. Trusted Service."

Brits Solutions began with a straightforward observation: most businesses in Gurgaon needed reliable computing hardware for finite windows of time — a project, a hiring surge, an event, a training batch — but were forced into buying equipment that sat idle the moment the need passed.

What started as a small fleet of laptops loaned out to a handful of local startups has grown into a full-service IT rental operation covering laptops, desktops, monitors, peripherals, networking gear and complete office setups — all backed by same-day delivery and on-site support across the NCR region.

The philosophy hasn't changed: keep the hardware honest, the pricing transparent, and the turnaround fast enough that "we need it tomorrow" is never a problem.
